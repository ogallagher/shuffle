Description

Shuffle is an original online strategy board game.
It requires skill, foresight, patience, and focus.
Size: 2 to 4 players.

Rules

Each player is given 6 pieces at random locations on the board.
Every piece can move one space at a time, in directions that vary based on piece type.
The 6 types are as follows, with the piece's movement ("cardinal" means "horizontal and vertical") paired with its name:
CIRCLE: anywhere
SQUARE: cardinal
TRIANGLE: diagonal
STAR: anywhere
PLUS: cardinal
EX: diagonal
On a turn, EVERY player moves ALL the pieces on its team.
Since all pieces move at the same time, there are collisions, and what happens therefore is determined by the involved pieces' types.
The upper three pieces are block pieces, and the lower three are attack pieces. The pieces are listed above in order from weakest to strongest by type. (TRIANGLE = strongest block; EX = strongest attack)
The following describe collision scenarios:
BLOCK: block[A]-attack[B] = B is sent back.
SHOVE: block[A]-block[B] = if A is stronger, B is shoved. Otherwise, A is shoved.
TAKE: attack[A]-attack[B] = if A is stronger, B is removed. Otherwise, A is removed.
STICK: same[A]-same[B] = if A and B are the same type, both are sent back.
SQUASH: any[A]-any[B] = if in one of the collisions above, A is sent to a space occupied by another piece B, A is removed.
Reading the collision scenarios, the STAR piece definitely seems like the worst piece on the board. However, if you take a piece (through a SQUASH) with the STAR, it can become a:
CAPTAIN: anywhere
The CAPTAIN is the seventh piece type, which is the strongest attack piece on the board.

End

Players are eliminated when they have less than 4 pieces remaining.
The game is over when all but one player is eliminated.

Start

Click on the game at the bottom of this page to initiate it.
You will be prompted to give a name for yourself. Click on ENTER NAME and do so; then press the play button.
Next, either choose an existing game (diamond buttons on the right) or a preferred game size (the numbers at the bottom).
When the game starts, the board is shown. Your pieces are BLUE.
Pick where you want them to move by clicking them. When you've finished, press the play button at the upper right corner.
When all players are done, the turn will be realized, and you will be able to move your pieces again when all players have seen the turn.
If you need to quit at any time, press the stop button in the lower right corner.

Notes

To see information on who is playing in your game, and to reveal the stop button, move the mouse/tap outside of the board.
Piece movement: "anywhere" means "to any adjacent space"; "cardinal" means "up, down, right, and left"; "diagonal" means "up-right, down-right, down-left, and up-left".
Be wary of possible SQUASHes. You can be SQUASHed by any piece, and if a STAR SQUASHes another piece, the STAR is converted into a CAPTAIN.
Pieces that are shoved off the board are eliminated.
