Shuffle

Author: Owen Gallagher

Description

Shuffle is an original online strategy board game.
It requires skill, foresight, patience, and focus.
Size: 2 to 4 players.

Rules

Each player is given 6 pieces at random locations on the board.
Every piece can move one space at a time, in directions that vary based on piece type.
The six types are as follows, with the piece's movement paired with its name:
CIRCLE: anywhere
SQUARE: cardinal
TRIANGLE: diagonal
STAR: anywhere
PLUS: cardinal
EX: diagonal
On a turn, EVERY player moves ALL the pieces on its team.
Since all pieces move at the same time, there are collisions, and what happens therefore is determined by the involved pieces' types.
The upper three pieces are block pieces, and the lower three are attack pieces. The pieces are listed above in order from fastest to slowest (weakest to strongest).
The following describe collision scenarios:
BLOCK: block[A]-attack[B] = B is sent back
SHOVE: block[A]-block[B] = if A is slower, B is shoved. Otherwise, A is shoved
TAKE: attack[A]-attack[B] = if A is slower, B is removed. Otherwise, A is removed
STICK: same[A]-same[B] = if A and B are the same type, both are sent back
SQUASH: any[A]-any[B] = if in one of the collisions above, A is sent to a space occupied by another piece B, A is removed.

End

Players are eliminated when they have less than 4 pieces remaining.
The game is over when all but one player is eliminated.

Start

Click on the game below to initiate it.
You will be prompted to give a name for yourself. Click on ENTER NAME and do so; then press the play button.
Next, either choose an existing game (diamond buttons on the right) or a preferred game size (the numbers at the bottom).
When the game starts, the board is shown. Your pieces are red.
Pick where you want them to move by clicking them. When you've finished, press the play button at the upper right corner.
When all players are done, the turn will be realized, and you will be able to move your pieces again when all players have seen the turn.
If you need to quit at any time, press the stop button in the lower right corner.
